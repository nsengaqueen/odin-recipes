Imvange

Description

Imvange is a local Rwandan food but also popular in the east african region.It is a main meal consisting mainly of potatoes mixed either with beans ,peas or other veggies of one's liking it is consumed in most household as lunch or diner and is quick fix enjoyed by many people accross Rwanda as it very affordable to everyone and does not take long to prepare.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Beans or Peas
  • Tomatoes
  • onions
  • Eggplants

Steps

  1. boil 3 cups of beans for 3hours
  2. pour 2l of water in a pot with potatoes in it
  3. after 30 minutes add beans to the potatoes
  4. after 5minutes add all the ingredients prepared
  5. wait for 10 more minutes and add sugar and stir for everything to mix
  6. if there is any water left filter it and now it is done
  7. Enjoy!
